## Ethereum Basics

### Ether Currency Units

* Ether
    - Main currency of Ethereum
    - Represented as an unsigned integer value(uint)
* Wei
    -  One ether is 1 quintillion wei (1 * 1018 or 1,000,000,000,000,000,000)
